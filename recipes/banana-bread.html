Banana Bread Recipe

Description

This recipe makes a savory banana bread

Ingredients

  • All-Purpose Flour (1.5 Cups)
  • Granulated Sugar (3/4 Cup)
  • Salt (.5 tsp)
  • Baking Soda (1 tsp)
  • Vanilla Extract (1/2 tsp)
  • Vanilla Flavored Protein Powder (1 Scoop)
  • Softened, Unsalted Butter (.5 Cups)
  • Eggs (2 Large)
  • RIPE Bananas (3 EA)
  • Chopped Walnuts
  • Chocolate Chips

Procedure

  1. Preheat oven to 350 Degrees.
  2. Grease and flour a bread loaf pan or Pot.
  3. (Optional) Lightly roast walnuts on a skillet, continously stirring to avoid burning. Remove and let cool.
  4. In the mixing bowl, mix together room temperature butter and granulated sugar
  5. Mash the bananas with a fork until it has an applesauce texture and add them to the bowl.
  6. Add 2 eggs to the bowl and mix until homeogeneous.
  7. In a separate bowl, whisk together:
    1. All-Purpose Flour (1.5 Cups)
    2. Baking Soda (1 tsp)
    3. Salt (.5 tsp)
    Once complete, add to the original bowl batter.
  8. Add .5 tsp of vanilla extract and 1 scoop of vanilla protein powder and mix together.
  9. (Optional) Mix in chopped walnuts if desired.
  10. Pour batter into the previously prepared loaf pan and bake at 350F for 55-60 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  11. After removing, let bread rest for 10 min in the pan/pot before transferring to a wire rack or paper towel to cool.
  12. Enjoy!!
