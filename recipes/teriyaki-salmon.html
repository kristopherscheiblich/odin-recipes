Teriyaki Salmon

Description

This recipe makes an amazing umami Teriyaki Salmon with baked broccoli and potatoes.

Ingredients

  • Salmon Filets (De-skin & De-Bone)
  • Fresh Ginger (1/3 Cup)
  • Teriyaki Sauce or Soy Sauce with 1/4 Cup Sugar (.5 - 1 Cup)
  • Rosemary (optional)
  • Thyme (optional)
  • Lemongrass (optional)
  • Spring/Green Onion (optional)
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt
  • Fresh Garlic (8 Cloves)
  • White Wine (.5 Cup)
  • Olive Oil

Procedure

Marinade

COMPILE MARINADE 2-8 HOURS BEFORE THE MEAL

  1. De-bone and de-skin salmon if needed.
  2. Grate Garlic until minced.
  3. Roughly Dice Ginger, doesn't need to be incredibly small.
  4. To a 1 Gal Ziploc Bag, add garlic, salt, pepper, and any optional ingredients from the ingredients list.
  5. Place salmon inside the ziploc bag and fill with teriyaki sauce until the salmon is barely immersed.
  6. Refrigerate and let marinate such that salmon is immersed in the mixture for 2-8 hours.

The Cook

  1. Cover the bottom of a pan with olive oil and warm it over med-high heat
  2. Place the fish in the hot pan (facing away from you to avoid splashing) and lay a couple spoonfuls of the marinade onto the salmon.
  3. Cover with lid and sear for 2 minutes. Remove the lid and sear for 2+ minutes or until the salmon sides have browning.
  4. Flip Salmon Filets and cook for 2+ minutes. Desired doneness is when the fish is just barely browned out along all sides.
  5. Enjoy!!
