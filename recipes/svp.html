Spicy Vodka Pasta

Description

This recipe makes a savory pasta sauce recipe in less than 35 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Red Onion (1 EA)
  • Tomato Paste (4 oz)
  • Garlic (4 cloves)
  • Vodka (1 shot)
  • Protein+ Pasta (1 lb)
  • Chili Flakes (to taste)
  • Butter (1 tbsp)
  • Heavy Whipping Cream (1 Cup)
  • Parmesan (1.5 cups)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Olive Oil

Procedure

  1. Coat Pan in a healthy basin of olive oil and set to medium-low heat
  2. Dice 1 Red Onion and place inside the pan. Cook until translucent
  3. Once Translucent, add ~4 cloves of grated/minced garlic
  4. Boil pasta water and salt the heck outta it. Get to a rolling boil and put Pasta in for the suggested time on the box.
  5. Add Tomato Paste and mix until relatively homogeneous. Cook until tomato paste color is glazed/darkened.
  6. Add Vodka shot to deglaze and cook for a few minutes to burn off the alcohol.
  7. Add Heavy Whipping Cream to the mixture.
  8. Shred Parmesan and add to the sauce. Mix until homogeneous and melted.
  9. Add Butter to the sauce and mix until homogeneous.
  10. Add Chili Flakes, Salt, Pepper, and Italian Seasoning to taste
  11. Strain Pasta after the alotted time has passed and mix pasta with the sauce in a large pot/bowl.
  12. Enjoy!!
